Author Guidelines
Please read the guidelines carefully before submitting. Manuscripts must be original, unpublished, and conform to ethical standards.

Manuscript Structure

  1. Title
  2. Authors and affiliations
  3. Abstract (150–250 words)
  4. Keywords (3–6)
  5. Introduction
  6. Related Work
  7. Methods
  8. Results and Discussion
  9. Conclusion
  10. References
