Article Processing Charges (APC)

For the launch phase, IJCAI charges no APC. This is to encourage high-quality submissions and build the journal's initial archive.

Future APC Policy

APC may be introduced later to support DOI registration, platform hosting, and editorial operations. Any APC changes will be announced in advance.
