Aims & Scope

IJCAI publishes original research and review articles covering core AI & computing topics and interdisciplinary applications that apply computing and AI to other fields.

  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Cybersecurity & Privacy
  • Computer Vision & NLP
  • Cloud, Edge & High-Performance Computing

Interdisciplinary Topics

  • AI in Healthcare, Finance, Education
  • Computational Social Science
  • AI Ethics, Governance & Explainability
  • Blockchain & Trust Systems
  • Smart Cities & IoT Applications

Article Types

Original research, review articles, short communications, technical reports and case studies.
